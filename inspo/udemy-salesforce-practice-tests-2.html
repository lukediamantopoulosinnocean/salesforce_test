Question 1:

A marketing manager has requested of their email marketing specialist to implement some techniques on their subject line to increase open rates.

Which two best practices should the email marketing specialist use to make the subject line more effective? (Choose 2 answers)   

Explanation

According to CAN-SPAM, it is best practice for the subject line to be relevant to the info within the email as deceitfully getting a customer to click on an email is unethical. It is important to A/B test the effectiveness of different subject lines to maximise customer satisfaction and to determine which subject line draws out the readers attention more effectively for the best open rates.
